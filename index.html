Established in 1990, Wayne Hodder Surveys is a professional land surveying and survey consulting firm operating out of Gander, Newfoundland and Labrador.  Please take the time to browse our web site to familiarize yourself with all of our services.  Twenty years in the business, Wayne Hodder Surveys has developed a reputation for unparalleled expertise, attention to detail and strong client relationships.

 

Our services include, but not limited to, surveys for real estate closings, large farms, industrial and commercial projects, subdivisions, townhomes, condominiums, the staking of utility easements, curb and gutters, and building layouts for contractors.  

Projects include preparation of subdivision design work and plans, topographic maps, maps of property surveys and real property reports for property closings.
